As a celebration of enlightening parentheses,
Maria Grazia Chiuri presents an original new wardrobe
on which flowers and birds flourish. A dreamy evocation
of a winter garden at once mysterious and spell-bindingly
scintillating, delicate sketches in shades of pink and green
illustrating the vital force of the plant world adorn
dresses, pants, and coats along with bags and accessories,
sublimating them with fresh poetry. A bucolic interlude.
